My Journey



Birth (August 6, 1998) Born and raised in the mountains of California, with my parents and my little brother, who also just recently got out of the Marine Corps after 4 years of service. We spent our childhood skiing and snowboarding for physical education class since we lived up in the mountains. It still makes me nostalgic for snow.
Graduated middle school as salutatorian. Graduated high school within top percentile. Moved high schools right before senior year, making my high school career a bit of a challenge. The type of kid to take 10 Advanced Placement classes throughout my 4 years. I went on to pursue one semester in college, but ultimately felt called to do something other than school, opening the door for military service consideration. Middle School/High School (2009-2016)
Military Service (2017-2022) Joined the United States Marine Corps in May of 2017. I was sent to boot camp for the next three months in Parris Island, then to additional training over the next year in Jacksonville, NC, and Pensacola, FL. I trained for my job as an Air Traffic Control Communications Technician, where we were responsible for setting up and maintaining radio networks for controllers to safely help aircraft land and take off. I spent a total of 5 years in the Marine Corps before deciding it was time to pursue a new adventure on the outside.

Now that I am out of the military, I am ready to see where life and the tech world takes me, as long as my husband, my dogs, and my friends are willing to be by my side through all of the trial and error. Fuuuuuuture
